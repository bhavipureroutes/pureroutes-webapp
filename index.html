About Pure Routes

Our Journey

PureRoutes Retail India Private Limited was incorporated with a bold vision to streamline and optimize B2B supply chains in India's underserved markets. Formally established in February 2025, we evolved from the high-performing proprietorship firm Kanak Enterprises, founded in December 2023.

Kanak Enterprises addressed the critical challenge of consistent, reliable supply of groceries and vegetables to construction companies in remote and Tier-3 regions. As a trusted sub-vendor to major infrastructure companies, we built our reputation serving 4 major clients across 8 states and 15+ remote locations.

Our remarkable first-year performance, generating INR 2.59 Crores in revenue, demonstrated the scalability of our model. Recognizing the systemic gaps in supply chains across India's non-metro geographies, we restructured as PureRoutes to attract institutional backing and expand our operational scope.

Pure Routes is uniquely positioned to bridge the gap between institutional food buyers and fragmented rural supply chains, using technology and operational intelligence to bring consistency, affordability, and quality to India's Tier 2 and Tier 3 restaurant economy.
